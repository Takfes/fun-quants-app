### Notation

- Leverage 1:100 => 100 x
- $Margin = Leverage * Position$; where Leverage = 1/100
- $Borrowed = (1 - Leverage) * Position$
- $Position = Margin + Borrowed$
- Margin aka Collateral
- $Balance / Equity = Margin + Free Margin$
